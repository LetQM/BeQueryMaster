1번 :상품 별 오프라인 매출 구하기
- SELECT PRODUCT_CODE,
  CASE
      WHEN offline_sale_id is null then 0
      ELSE SUM(SALES_AMOUNT) * PRICE
  END as SALES
  FROM PRODUCT p
  LEFT OUTER JOIN OFFLINE_SALE os ON p.PRODUCT_ID = os.PRODUCT_ID
  GROUP BY PRODUCT_CODE
  ORDER BY SALES DESC , PRODUCT_CODE asc

2번 :재구매가 일어난 상품과 회원 리스트 구하기
- SELECT USER_ID,	PRODUCT_ID FROM ONLINE_SALE
  GROUP BY USER_ID, PRODUCT_ID
  HAVING count(*) > 1
  ORDER BY USER_ID ASC, PRODUCT_ID DESC

3번 :진료과별 총 예약 횟수 출력하기
- SELECT MCDP_CD as '진료과코드', count(*) as '5월예약건수' FROM APPOINTMENT
  WHERE month(APNT_YMD) = 5
  GROUP BY MCDP_CD
  ORDER BY 5월예약건수 ASC, 진료과코드 ASC
